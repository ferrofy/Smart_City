This Document Details Every Standalone Feature Our System Brings To The Smart City Ecosystem.

## 1. Smart Traffic Control 🚦
* **Automated Flow Analysis**: Detect Vehicle Density Using Advanced AI Vision.
* **Dynamic Timings**: Adjust Traffic Light Timing Automatically To Prevent Jams.

## 2. Emergency Vehicle Detection 🚑
* **Immediate Identification**: Detect Ambulance And Other Emergency Vehicles.
* **Green Corridor Creation**: Immediately Give Green Signal To The Emergency Lane.
* **Network Alerting**: Notify Nearby Signals For Seamless Traffic Clearances.

## 3. Accident Detection 🚨
* **Crash Recognition**: Detect Sudden Collisions Or Unusual Vehicle Motions.
* **Automated Dispatch**: Immediately Send Alert To Ambulance, Police, And Hospitals.
* **Traffic Rerouting**: Control Traffic Lights To Keep Responders Clearing The Way.

## 4. Waste Detection And Management ♻️
* **Smart Monitoring**: Detect Overflowing Garbage Areas Automatically.
* **Automated Classification**: Classify Waste Types For Better Processing.
* **Cleanup Dispatch**: Send Alert To The Designated Cleaning Authority Automatically.

## 5. Crime Prediction And Monitoring 🔥
* **Historical Data Analysis**: Use Past Crime Data To Enhance Patrol Recommendations.
* **Heatmap Generation**: Generate A Live Heatmap Of High-Risk Or Dangerous Areas.
* **Crowd Control**: Detect Suspicious Gatherings And Alert The Police If Needed.

## 6. Smart Energy System ⚡
* **Adaptive Lighting**: Use Ambient Light Sensors To Dynamically Control Street Lights.
* **Power Management**: Turn Off Lights When No Humans Or Vehicles Are Needed.
* **Green Energy**: Simulate Solar Panel Energy Usage For Eco-Friendly Demonstrations.

## 7. Smart Parking System 🅿️
* **Space Tracking**: Detect Empty Parking Slots In Real-Time.
* **Driver Dashboard**: Show Direct Availability On The User Dashboard To Prevent Idle Cruising.
